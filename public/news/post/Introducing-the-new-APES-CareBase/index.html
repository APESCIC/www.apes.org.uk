Introducing APES CareBase: a new home for animal welfare data.

This legacy article keeps the APES CareBase announcement reachable while directing readers to APES Newsroom for current updates.

Legacy article APES CareBase APES Newsroom
Open APES Newsroom Visit APES CareBase

APES announced that the long-standing database previously known as NED had evolved into APES CareBase, broadening its focus from exotic species only to animal welfare data across species, settings and care environments.

The public story emphasised consistent welfare records, evidence-based decision making, transparency and collaboration. It also stated that registration had opened for the platform.
